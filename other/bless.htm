BLESSINGS AND CURSES

Give, remove or adjust blessings and curses on a specific hero.

You can edit attributes of EVERY curse and setup unusued ones with CR receivver. However, hard-coded effects (mostly) cannot be changed.

 
!!HE#:Y$1/$2/$3/$4; Place a blessing or curse on the hero number of # (see Format H - each hero has a unique number in the game).
!!HE#1/#2/#3:Y$1/$2/$3/$4; Place a blessing or curse on the hero at x,y,l coordinates
   #1 = x
   #2 = y
   #3 = level (0-ground, 1-Underground)
!!HE-1:Y$1/$2/$3/$4; Place a blessing or curse on the hero who activated the trigger. Using this format simplifies things greatly as you do not need to keep changing the coordinates for every !!HE command if you move the trigger event or object. 


Options
Y$1/$2/$3/$4 Add/change/remove a curse.
   $1 - number of curse from 1 to 100
   $2 - the power of curse (not used for Blinding and Lock)
   $3 - the length in days of the curse
   $4 - what to do
      1 = set
      2 = add/sub
      0 = del
Special case: HE:Y0/0/0/3 - remove all curses/blessings from the hero.

Comments:
Every hero can have only one curse of each type. So when you add/sub (sub is addition of negative value), that means that the power and the length will be changed to a sum of old and new values. If you del the curse, the power and length parameters do not matter.
Y2/$1/$2/#

Slot lock curse
   $1 - number of slot to lock (-1=random slot).
   $2 - the length in days of the curse.
   # - set(1) add(2) or del(0).

Y$1/$2/$3

New HE:Y syntax
   $1 - number of curse from 1 to 100
   $2 - the power of curse (not used for Blinding and Lock)
   $3 - length of curse (in days)

HE:Y now supports all syntax variations, so 4th parameter is usually unneeded.
Examples:
!!HE-1:Yv1/v2/v3 - set curse.
!!HE-1:Yv1/dv2/dv3 - add/sub curse.
!!HE-1:Yv1/0/0 - delete curse.
!!HE-1:Yv1/?v2/?v3 - get curse.
The only case when 4th is needed:
HE:Y0/0/0/3 - remove all curses/blessings from the hero.
